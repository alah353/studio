# **App Name**: SwiftRoute Logistics

## Core Features:

- Homepage Overview: Presents a concise overview of services emphasizing speed, security, and international coverage.
- About Us: Showcases the company's experience, mission, vision, and values, with a brief introduction to the team.
- Services Showcase: Details offered services like land, sea, and air transport, integral logistics, tracking, and warehousing.
- Real-time Shipment Tracking Tool: The AI tool determines and presents current shipment status, route, and estimated delivery time based on the tracking number, using predictive algorithms for dynamic updates.
- Fleet and Tech Display: Details the types of vehicles used and highlights advanced tracking (GPS, real-time traceability) and logistic innovations employed.
- Blog/News Section: A dedicated space for industry news, logistic trends, and useful tips to keep customers informed and engaged.
- Contact Form and Info: Provides easy access to a contact form and lists various contact details, emphasizing personalized attention.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep Blue (#1A5276) to convey trust and reliability in logistics.
- Background color: Light Blue (#D4E6F1), offering a clean and calming visual base.
- Accent color: Vibrant Orange (#E67E22) to highlight calls to action and key information.
- Body font: 'PT Sans', a modern and readable sans-serif font for body text.
- Headline font: 'Space Grotesk', a techy sans-serif font for headlines.
- Use clear and modern icons that represent each service category and logistics feature.
- Subtle loading animations and transitions to enhance user experience during tracking updates and form submissions.
